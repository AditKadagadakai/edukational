About Edukational

Edukational is an unique initiative to make quality education accessible to all the children from different financial backgrounds.


It provides a platform through which students can access wide range of concepts and improve their learning. The language is easily understandable. Students can clarify their doubts there and then on a chat platform.

Our endeavour is to connect the same with Government sponsored programs such as Skill India, Rashtriya Madyamik Shiksha Abhiyan and other future initiatives . This will provide greater accessability to students across India.
